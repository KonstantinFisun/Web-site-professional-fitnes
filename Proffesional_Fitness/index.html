Our Gym is an incorporated, not-for-profit organisation, with a constitution. Sport is one of the four organisations on the campus which receives an annual allocation of Student Services and Amenities Fee contributed by our Students. Other major sources of income include non-student member fees, facility usage charges and short courses. The objectives of the Sport are to promote and provide a diverse and exciting range of professionally-provided, affordable sporting and physical recreation participation opportunities for all of our members.
To enhance your university experience by offering health, fitness and leisure activities to make your time at the a whole lot more energetic, invigorating and enjoyable. Essential to this purpose, we encourage and assist in the formation among members of sporting and physical recreation clubs, and to support and co-ordinate the activities of clubs, so they can provide exceptional experiences for their members, both on and off the competition arena!

Supervision and professional advice

During your first visit to the gym, an instructor will help you find your way. One or more instructors are present to help you with your training, answer your questions and to secure a comfortable and safe environment. Ask the instructors to help you at any time, as they can show you how to train both safe and effective, inspire you with new exercises and keep you motivated. For a personal training plan (free of additional costs) you can book an appointment with an instructor at the Gym desk.
